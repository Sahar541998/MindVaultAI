## Description

MindVault AI is your personal thought organizer powered by Apple Intelligence.

Speak or type your thoughts, and let on-device AI automatically categorize them into topics, generate concise summaries, and keep everything searchable. All your data stays private — processed entirely on your device and synced securely via iCloud.

**Key Features:**

• Voice & Text Input — Record thoughts by speaking or typing. Voice transcription happens on-device using Apple's Speech framework.

• AI-Powered Organization — Apple Intelligence automatically suggests the right topic for each thought, or proposes a new one when needed.

• Smart Summaries — Get AI-generated summaries for each topic that evolve as you add more entries.

• Ask AI — Ask questions about your entries and get intelligent, contextual answers powered by Apple Intelligence.

• iCloud Sync — Access your thoughts seamlessly across iPhone and Mac.

• Privacy First — All AI processing happens on-device. No data is ever sent to external servers.

• Beautiful Design — Clean, modern interface with dark and light mode support.

## App Review Notes

This app uses on-device Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models framework) for text categorization and summarization. These features require a device with Apple Silicon. On devices without Apple Intelligence, the AI features gracefully degrade — users can still manually organize and manage their thoughts.

The app requires microphone and speech recognition permissions only when the user chooses to record a thought via voice. No audio is stored or transmitted externally.

All data sync is handled via CloudKit through the user's personal iCloud account.
