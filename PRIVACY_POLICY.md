# Privacy Policy for MindVault AI

**Last Updated: February 28, 2026**

## Overview

MindVault AI ("the App") is developed by Sahar Levy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

## Data Collection

MindVault AI does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third parties.

### What stays on your device

- **Your thoughts and entries**: All text you record or type is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **Voice recordings**: Audio is processed on-device by Apple's Speech framework for transcription. No audio is stored or transmitted to external servers.
- **AI processing**: All AI features use Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Your data is never sent to external AI services.

### iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: Required for voice-to-text transcription. Audio is processed on-device only.
- **Speech Recognition**: Required to convert your spoken words to text. Processing happens on-device.

These permissions are only used when you actively choose to record a thought. You can revoke them at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security.

## Third-Party Services

MindVault AI does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device (and in your personal iCloud account, if enabled) until you choose to delete it. Deleting the App removes all local data. iCloud data can be managed through your iCloud settings.

## Children's Privacy

MindVault AI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not contain any age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in the App and on this page with an updated date.
